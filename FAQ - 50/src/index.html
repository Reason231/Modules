Facts
What is the tallest mountain on Earth?
Mount Everest is the tallest mountain in the world, standing at 8,848 meters (29,029 feet) above sea level.
Can sound travel through space?
No, sound cannot travel through the vacuum of space as it needs a medium like air, water, or solids to propagate.
What percentage of the human body is water?
Around 60% of the human adult body is made up of water. The brain and heart are composed of 73% water.
How fast does the Earth orbit the Sun?
The Earth orbits the Sun at an average speed of about 30 kilometers per second, or roughly 108,000 kilometers per hour.
